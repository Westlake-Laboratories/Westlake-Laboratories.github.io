Westlake

We are a group of young people indulged in nostalgic retro artworks & art styles. By experimenting with different media and techniques we try to look back upon the true essentials of retro styles and by inspectiong those styles we try to reveal idea that could have deep revelations about today's art and design.

So far we have only one member and we are not recruiting new members now because of the difficulty to have a relatively fair and satisfying rule to deal with copyright issues.
